Irresistibly Tangy Sweet and Sour Chicken

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 1 lb (450 g) boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup cornstarch
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

For the Sweet and Sour Sauce:

  • 1 cup pineapple juice (from a can of pineapple chunks, reserve chunks)
  • 1/3 cup rice vinegar or white vinegar
  • 1/3 cup ketchup
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch mixed with 2 teaspoons water

For the Stir-Fry:

  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 green bell pepper, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 medium onion, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 2 teaspoons grated fresh ginger
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced

Directions

  1. Prepare the Chicken:
    • Season the chicken pieces with salt and pepper.
    • Dredge each piece of chicken in cornstarch, then dip in the beaten eggs, and dredge again in cornstarch. Set aside.
  2. Fry the Chicken:
    • Heat about 1 inch of vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
    • Once the oil is hot, fry the chicken pieces in batches until golden brown and cooked through, about 4-5 minutes per batch. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.
  3. Make the Sauce:
    • In a medium bowl, combine pineapple juice, vinegar, ketchup, brown sugar, and soy sauce. Stir until well mixed.
    • In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch and water to make a slurry.
  4. Cook the Vegetables:
    • In a large pan or wok, heat a small amount of oil over medium heat.
    • Add the ginger and garlic, and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
    • Add the bell peppers and onion, and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until they start to soften.
    • Add the pineapple chunks and stir-fry for another 2 minutes.
  5. Combine Everything:
    • Pour the sauce mixture into the pan with the vegetables. Bring to a simmer.
    • Stir in the cornstarch slurry and cook until the sauce thickens, about 1-2 minutes.
    • Add the fried chicken pieces to the pan and toss to coat
