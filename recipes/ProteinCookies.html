Delicious and Nutritious Protein-Packed Cookies

Description

Treat yourself to the perfect post-workout snack or guilt-free treat with these Irresistibly Chewy and Delicious Protein Cookies! Each bite offers a delightful blend of soft, chewy texture and rich, satisfying flavor, packed with wholesome ingredients and a generous dose of protein. Whether you're aiming to satisfy your sweet tooth or fuel your fitness journey, these cookies are designed to fit seamlessly into your lifestyle. Easy to make and even easier to enjoy, they are sure to become a favorite in your kitchen. With a perfect balance of taste and nutrition, these cookies are not just a snack, but a step towards a healthier you.

NOTE FOR CRUNCHY-COOKIE-LOVERS: I have included some tips at the end for those that prefer crunchy cookies rather than chewy.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1 cup oat flour (or finely ground oats)
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder (whey or plant-based)
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup creamy almond butter (or peanut butter)
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup mini dark chocolate chips (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (optional, e.g., almonds, walnuts)

Directions

  1. Preheat the Oven:
    • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Mix Dry Ingredients:
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine almond flour, oat flour, protein powder, baking soda, and salt. Stir until well mixed.
  3. Prepare Wet Ingredients:
    • In a separate bowl, mix together the almond butter, melted coconut oil, honey or maple syrup, applesauce, and vanilla extract until smooth and well combined.
  4. Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients:
    • Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until a thick dough forms. If the dough is too dry, add a bit more applesauce or a splash of milk (dairy or non-dairy) until the desired consistency is reached.
  5. Add Mix-Ins:
    • Fold in the mini dark chocolate chips and chopped nuts, if using.
  6. Form the Cookies:
    • Scoop out dough using a tablespoon or cookie scoop and place on the prepared baking sheet. Flatten each scoop slightly with your fingers or the back of a spoon to form a cookie shape.
  7. Bake:
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden. The cookies will still be soft in the center but will firm up as they cool.
  8. Cool and Enjoy:
    • Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Additional Notes:

  • Storage: Store the cookies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days, or in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks. They can also be frozen for longer storage.
  • Customization: Feel free to customize these cookies by adding your favorite mix-ins such as dried fruit, seeds, or different types of nuts.
  • Nutrition Information: These cookies are high in protein and healthy fats, making them a great option for a post-workout snack or a healthy treat. Adjust the sweetness to your preference by varying the amount of honey or maple syrup used.

Note for Crunchy Cookies:

If you prefer your protein cookies to be crunchy rather than chewy, follow these tips:

  • Baking Time: Extend the baking time by 3-5 minutes. Keep an eye on the cookies and remove them from the oven when they are a deep golden brown around the edges.
  • Omit Applesauce: Replace the applesauce with additional coconut oil or a bit more nut butter to reduce the moisture content.
  • Use Less Moisture: Reduce the amount of honey or maple syrup slightly.
  • Cool Completely: Allow the cookies to cool completely on the baking sheet before transferring them to a wire rack. This will help them set and become crispier.
  • Storage: Store the cookies in an airtight container at room temperature. If you live in a humid climate, you might want to add a small packet of silica gel to the container to keep the cookies crisp.

By following these adjustments, you can enjoy a delicious, crunchy version of these protein cookies!
